“Access”

is a term frequently used in the art world. For many—especially those in their 20s and 30s— hurdles like knowledge, finances and taste can make acquiring art feel unattainable or inaccessible.

Currently, art is primarily collected by those who are older and have the financial means, time and resources.

There is limited space that caters to collectors in their 20s and 30s due to obvious reasons, but this is changing. Future generations of tastemakers will reshape the art world.

We are cultivating a community of people who love art and share an interest in collecting. Whether you have already begun your art collecting journey or may start in 10 to 20 years, let’s embark on this adventure together.

We invite you to join us if you find yourself:

  • a) hoping to share and discuss the art you want to collect
  • b) wanting to exist in the art world in new ways
  • c) becoming curious and eager to explore but not quite sure where to start
